About Research Week

The De La Salle Medical and Health Sciences Institute (DLSMHSI) Research Week is our premier annual event celebrating the spirit of inquiry, innovation, and academic excellence within our institution.

Under the theme "Expanding Horizons, Strengthening Research Excellence," this week-long celebration brings together faculty, students, researchers, and industry professionals to share groundbreaking research, foster collaboration, and advance knowledge in medical and health sciences.

Join us from November 10-14, 2025 at the Villarosa Hall, DLSMHSI Angelo King Medical Center for five days of inspiring presentations, innovative research showcases, and meaningful academic discourse.
